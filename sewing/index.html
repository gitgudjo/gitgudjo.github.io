Creating clothing: an example

This is a visual tutorial on how to make a pair of shorts. These skills can be used for other sewing projects as all of the skills are transferable. The first step is to use a template to base your piece off of. You can use another pair of shorts or buy a template in store. Fold the material in half to cut both sides of the clothing at the same time. Use pins to keep the template steady. Use the sewing machine to outline the pieces you cut and prevent the edge of the material from falling apart. This will not be visible in the final product. Cut out the four pieces needed to make the pockets. Inside,outside left and right.
