Embroidery: tutorial and example

This will be a quick tutorial on emroidery, showing you how to embroider any design. The first step is to get a template to follow. You can draw something onto the fabric or print using transfer film. Cut a length of thread and tie a double or triple knot at the end big enough to avoid it slipping through the fabric. Using an embroidery hoop helps keep the fabric taut when working. The next steps are self explanatory and I will let the images speak for themselves. Tying knots at the back of the embroidery helps make it look nicer and prevents it from loosening.
